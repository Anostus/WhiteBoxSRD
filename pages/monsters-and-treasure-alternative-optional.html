Monsters and Treasure (Alternative/Optional)

Monsters and Treasure

Monsters usually carry 1d6 gold pieces in treasure per Hit Die. To determine if a monster has any magic items, roll 1d20 per HD of the Monster. If any number rolled is equal to or less than the monster’s HD, then roll on Table 6-1: Magic Items to determine a magic item carried by the monster. Some monsters hoard no treasure (like giant centipedes), while others hoard a great amount of treasure (like dragons). The Referee can modify the amount of treasure found based on logic and narrative consistency.

Magic Items

Whenever PCs discover a magic item, the Referee rolls on Table 6-1: Magic Items to determine what item is found.
