Monster category removal and link cleanup
=========================================

Summary
-------
This pass deletes the monster subcategory layer entirely and migrates the final shared rules/descriptive text into the relevant individual monster entries.

Changes made
------------
- Deleted monsters/subcategories/ and all remaining subcategory pages, including the subcategory index.
- Removed Monster Subcategories from site navigation and body copy.
- Migrated the former Bear, Demon, Dracomander, Dragon, Eel, Golem, Human, Living Statue, Lycanthrope, and Shrew category notes into their individual member pages before the source note/footer.
- Added the Dragon Rules block, including dragon age and breath weapon tables, to every dragon entry.
- Added the Dragon Rules block to every dracomander entry as well.
- Added/updated See Also sections for every member of the former Bear, Demon/Demon, Dracomander, Dragon, Eel, Golem, Human, Living Statue, Lycanthrope, and Shrew groups.
- Treated Demon and Infernal as a single See Also group, with an explicit note that demons and infernals are equivalent lower-planar creatures in this SRD.
- Deleted the redundant Field Guide infernal duplicate pages:
  - demon-balor-bfomnibus.html
  - demon-succubus-bfomnibus.html
  - demon-lemure-bfomnibus.html
  - demon-imp-bfomnibus.html
- Removed the alternate-version notes from the White Box Demon pages that pointed to those deleted infernal duplicates.
- Deleted these additional redundant monster pages:
  - violet-fungus-bfomnibus.html
  - mold-deadly-yellow-bfomnibus.html
  - blink-dog-bfrpg.html
- Renamed the 36 HD Field Guide Kraken conversion to Kraken, Greater and renamed its file to kraken-greater-bfomnibus.html.
- Updated monster-index JSON, monster index HTML, iconic substitution links, alternate-version links, and all affected local hrefs.
- Replaced lingering generic links to the deleted Dragon subcategory page with plain Dragon text where the rulebook was using “dragon” as a saving throw/encounter/potion category rather than pointing to a specific monster.
