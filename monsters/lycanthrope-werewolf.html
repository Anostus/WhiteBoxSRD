Lycanthrope, Werewolf

Description

Werewolves are the traditional Lycanthropes seen in horror movies. They are often only affected by silver or magical weapons, and are often humanoid—except for during a full moon, and so on.

Lycanthrope Notes

Lycanthropes are were-creatures capable of assuming animal or hybrid forms, but these lycanthrope entries mix classic White Box lycanthropes with converted Basic Fantasy variants. Do not assume a single transmission or damage-immunity rule for every member.

Werebears, wererats, and werewolves list lycanthropy as a special ability; wererats also control rats and surprise opponents on 1–4 on 1d6. Wereboars and weretigers instead emphasize transformation and immunity to normal weapons, requiring magical or silver weapons to hit; weretigers are also exceptional trackers. Exact form-changing, infection, weapon immunity, and control abilities should be read from the individual monster entry.
