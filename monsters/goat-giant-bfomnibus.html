Description

Giant goats are simply very large versions of the common goat. They are often found in hilly or mountainous regions where giants live. They are quite aggressive if threatened, and if able to charge they gain a +4 bonus to damage.

Whenever 7 or more are encountered, the remainder will be young, having 1+1 or 2+1 hit dice (50% chance of either, but all will be the same) and doing 1d8 points of damage per attack.
