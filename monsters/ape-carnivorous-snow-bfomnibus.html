Description

Carnivorous Snow Apes are a larger variety of carnivorous ape with shaggy snow-white fur. They have long fangs for killing and tearing flesh. Frost giants often breed snow apes and keep them as pets. A carnivorous snow ape is difficult to see in ice or snow, and thus surprises on 1-4 on 1d6 in such conditions.
