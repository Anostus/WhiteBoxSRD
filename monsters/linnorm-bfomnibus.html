Linnorm

Armor Class
3 [16]

7

Bite or Claw (2d6)
Special

Move
12
HDE/XP
9/1,100

Description

A Linnorm, like a wyvern, is distantly related to a dragon. A linnorm resembles a large horned snake with a pair of clawed forelimbs.

A linnorm can breathe an 80-foot long, 30-foot wide cloud of fetid gas, much like a dragon's breath, for 7d8 points of damage. The bite of the linnorm is poisonous; those bitten must save vs. Poison or die. A linnorm is immune to all poisons.
