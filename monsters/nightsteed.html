Nightsteed

Armor Class
2 [17]
Hit Dice
5+5
Attacks
Flaming Hooves (2d6)
Special
Fearful Cry
Move
15 (18 when flying)
HDE/XP
5/240

Description

A nightsteed is a black horse with burning red eyes and a mane of fire. Its hooves also burn with hellfire and it can fly as easily as run. They attack with their flaming hooves, and once per day can let out a terrible cry that requires all who hear it to make a saving throw or flee in terror of the nightsteed for 1d6 rounds.
