Unicorn

2 [17]

4
Attacks
Hoof or horn
Special
See below
Move
24
HDE/XP
5/240

Description

Unicorns are generally shy and benevolent creatures, who will only allow a chaste maiden to approach them. They can teleport once per day to a distance of 360 feet, with a rider. When they charge, their horn can deal double damage. Unicorns are 25% resistant to magic. The unicorn’s horn has healing properties according to legend (the details of this,if any,are left to the Referee).There is considerable room to create variants of sorts: evil or flying unicorns, etc.
