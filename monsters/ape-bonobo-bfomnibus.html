Ape, Bonobo

Armor Class
7 [12]
Hit Dice
1-1
Attacks
Claw (1d6-1)
Special
None
Move
15
HDE/XP
1/15

Description

Bonobos are closely related to common chimpanzees, but they are much rarer. A bonobo is a less aggressive and more social member of the ape family.
