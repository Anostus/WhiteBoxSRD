Description

A Sea Cat is an aquatic hybrid having a feline head and a fish body. A typical sea cat is 12 feet long and weighs 800 pounds, and is sea green in color.

A sea cat attacks on sight, either for food or to defend its territory, and uses both claws and teeth. It displays amazing courage, fighting to the death even against creatures many times its size. A sea cat will use all of its attacks against a single opponent and none will stand on the sidelines, even if that means taking turns against a single prey and/or taking damage from other opponents. Upon scoring hits with both claw attacks, its bite hits automatically, dealing 3d6 points of rending damage.
