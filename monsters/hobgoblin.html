Description

Hobgoblins are simply large goblins, possibly a separate breed living apart from their smaller cousins. As a matter of the campaign’s flavoring, the Referee might choose to make hobgoblins the “fey” goblins of Irish legend, while regular goblins are the more Tolkien-style undergrounddwellers. Horse Horses are AC 7 [12], with riding horses having 2 HD and a Move of 24 and warhorses having 3 HD and a Move of 18. Draft horses have a Move of 12.
