Description

Dwarves are known for their War Rams, which are specifically bred for strength and aggression. It attacks with either its great horns (needing a short run or charge) or its hooves. Like the mundane breeds of ram, a war ram can negotiate rocky terrain with ease. The climbing speed listed above assumes its native terrain of rocky slopes and cliffsides; a war ram cannot climb walls, trees, or other objects in the traditional sense.
