Description

Giant shrews resemble giant rats, but are larger, being up to 6' long, and darker in color. They have a very fast metabolic rate and must eat almost constantly. Giant shrews are omnivorous, and aggressively defend their nests and the immediate territory around them.

Giant shrews move so swiftly that when a bite attack rolls a natural 19-20 they may immediately bite a second adjacent opponent.

A few giant shrew species (generally no more than 5% of those encountered) are venomous. The bite of such a giant shrew will kill the victim unless a save vs. Poison is made. A victim bitten twice in a round need only save once for that round, but of course will have to save again in subsequent rounds if bitten again.

Shrew Notes

Giant shrews resemble oversized rats, but they are larger, darker, ravenously hungry, and extremely territorial. Their speed no longer grants two automatic bites each round: when a bite attack rolls a natural 19–20, the shrew may immediately bite a second adjacent opponent.

Venomous giant shrews are uncommon variants, represented here by a separate individual entry. Their bite kills a victim that fails a save vs. Poison. A victim bitten twice in the same round need only save once for that round, but must save again in later rounds if bitten again.
