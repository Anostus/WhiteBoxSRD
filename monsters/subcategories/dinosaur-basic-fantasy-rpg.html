Dinosaur

Source: Consolidated subcategory; includes converted entries from Basic Fantasy RPG and Basic Fantasy Field Guide Omnibus where available.

Consolidated category: Former separate source-specific subcategories have been folded into this page. Monster entries use the site’s White Box-style stat block; source-specific or odd-man-out mechanics remain on individual monster pages.

This consolidated dinosaur subcategory collects converted entries from multiple Basic Fantasy-derived sources into a single index. Individual monster pages retain their complete statistics and any special rules.

For play at this site, these entries are presented with the White Box-style stat block used throughout the monster index. Any unusual mechanics from a specific source are kept with that creature’s individual entry rather than split into a separate source subcategory.
