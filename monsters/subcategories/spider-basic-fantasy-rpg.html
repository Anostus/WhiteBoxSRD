Source: Consolidated subcategory; includes converted entries from Basic Fantasy RPG and Basic Fantasy Field Guide Omnibus where available.

This consolidated spider subcategory collects converted entries from multiple Basic Fantasy-derived sources into a single index. Individual monster pages retain their complete statistics and any special rules.
