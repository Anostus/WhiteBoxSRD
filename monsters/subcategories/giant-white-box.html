Giant (White Box)

Source: Consolidated subcategory using White Box mechanics; includes converted entries from Basic Fantasy RPG and/or Basic Fantasy Field Guide Omnibus where available.

Consolidated category: Former separate source-specific subcategories have been folded into this page. Monster entries use the site’s White Box-style stat block; source-specific or odd-man-out mechanics remain on individual monster pages.

Additional converted giant variants are indexed here alongside the White Box giants. Any unusual giant-specific powers or tactics remain in the individual entries.

Giants are a staple of fantasy gaming, huge and dangerous creatures that often have a taste for human flesh. Most are not particularly intelligent.
