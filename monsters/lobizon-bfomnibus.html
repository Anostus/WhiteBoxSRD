Description

A Lobizon is a person cursed to become a malnourished, tail-less wolf-like creature of human size; it has humanoid ears, hands, and feet and is covered in very short fur. A lobizon is nocturnal and has Darkvision to 30 feet.

A lobizon lives by feeding on the offerings to the dead (from flowers to candles and even non-edible things); if none are found it will feed on the remains of the dead. Thus they are commonly sighted in graveyards where there can be as many as 3d6 lobizons. In combat a lobizon seeks to grab its opponent with both hands, and if successful will automatically lick it. Those licked by a lobizon must save vs. Spells or be cursed to become a lobizon in 2d6 days.
