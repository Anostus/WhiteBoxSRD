Manticore

Armor Class
4 [15]

6+4
Attacks
Bite (1d6+1), or Tail-spikes (1d6)
Special
Flight
Move
12/8(when flying)

8/800

Description

A horrid monster with bat wings, the face of a feral human, the body of a lion, and a tail tipped with 24 iron spikes. The manticore can hurl up to 6 of the iron spikes from its tail per round, at a maximum range of 180 feet.
