HDE/XP
4/120

Description

Dogboys are 5’ tall semi-humanoid versions of hell hounds that have learned to walk on their hind legs. They are cowardly bullies who will taunt victims and then flee to avoid getting hurt.

Like hell hounds, they choose to breath fire 1 in 6 times, producing a 10’x10’ code that does 3d6 damage.
