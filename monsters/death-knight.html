Description

These terrible undead knights are clad in black armor and wield terrible twohanded swords with great strength. In addition to increased damage, each time a death knight successfully strikes a foe with their sword the target loses one level. Death Knights are also immune to all spells of 5th level or lower.They can cast a Fireball once per day that inflicts 7d6 points of damage. They are immune to damage from mundane weapons and cannot be banished by a cleric. Demons Demons are creatures of the lower planes of existence, but they are occasionally encountered in places where they have been enslaved to serve as guardians by powerful Magic-users or Chaotic Clerics. The more intelligent varieties might also be interrupted while carrying out plots of their own. There is no meaningful game distinction between demons and devils.
