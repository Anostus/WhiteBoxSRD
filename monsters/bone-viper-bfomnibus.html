Bone Viper

Armor Class
6 [13] (special, see below)
Hit Dice
1
Attacks
Bite or Spit (1d3)
Special
poison, hold/grab, charm, sleep, undead
Move
12
HDE/XP
2/30

Description

The Bone Viper is an animated, undead snake skeleton, usually animated as a defender of some wizard's tower or evil temple. It is able to spit its magically potent poison up to 5 feet, and anyone hit by its spit attack must make a save vs. Poison or become blinded. However, its poison is far more dangerous when injected by a bite; anyone bitten must save vs. Poison or die within 1d3 rounds.

As undead, bone vipers may be Turned by a Cleric (as a skeleton), and have the usual undead immunity to Sleep, charm, and hold magic. Like humanoid skeletons they only take half damage from edged weapons and one single point of damage (plus any magical bonus) from small missile weapons like arrows or sling stones.
