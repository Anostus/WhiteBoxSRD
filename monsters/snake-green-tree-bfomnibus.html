Description

Green tree snakes are arboreal, non-venomous snakes reaching up to 5 feet in length. They are most at home in trees, preying on rodents, lizards, and birds. They are excellent climbers and are often found coiled around tree branches. Their bite is relatively harmless, though it may rarely cause a mild allergic reaction in some individuals.
