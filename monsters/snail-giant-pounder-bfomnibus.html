Description

A Giant Pounder Snail is the size of a large dog or pony. It is often heard from a distance, pounding the ground with a hard bony appendage to locate hollows and burrows to break into and then engulf any living matter therein. The appendage is shaped much like a war hammer or mace. Its mouth is little more than rough grinding surfaces, and do little more than 1d2 points of damage, but if provoked they will bite.
