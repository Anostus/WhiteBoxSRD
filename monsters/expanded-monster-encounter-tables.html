Expanded Monster Encounter Tables

These tables expand Chapter 7’s Underworld Encounter Levels and Wilderness Encounter Type tables to cover every monster currently in this SRD: the original White Box core monsters plus the converted Basic Fantasy RPG and Basic Fantasy Field Guide creatures. Basic Fantasy-derived creatures are marked with a dagger.

Need encounter size? After rolling a monster here, use Quick No. Appearing Rules (Optional) to estimate how many appear in a dungeon, wilderness, or lair scene.
