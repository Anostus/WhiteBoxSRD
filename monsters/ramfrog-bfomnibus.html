Description

A Ramfrog is a seemingly-normal frog except for 2 big ram-like horns on its heads. It tends to be inoffensive unless provoked or when its lair is invaded.

A ramfrog has supernaturally-strong hind legs, able to execute amazing jumps and can also propel towards opponents up to 90 feet away in order to headbutt them.
