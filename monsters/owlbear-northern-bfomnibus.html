Owlbear, Northern

Armor Class
4 [15]
Hit Dice
8
Attacks
Bite or Claw (2d6)
Special
hug, cold, immunity
Move
12 Fly 18
HDE/XP
8/800

Description

In a similar fashion to the standard owlbear, a Northern Owlbear mixes the qualities of a polar bear and snow owl. It tends to be substantially larger than the southern climate owlbear. On rare occasions a northern owlbear is hatched with large feathered wings. A northern owlbear fights much like a bear but is more aggressive. Also like a normal bear a northern owlbear must hit with both claws in order to deal the listed "hug" damage. As a polar climate inhabitant it is essentially immune to cold environmental effects and even extreme or magical cold causes half-damage. If a save is involved with such a cold-based effect, it receives a +4 bonus.
