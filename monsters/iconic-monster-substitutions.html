Iconic Monster Substitutions

This index maps familiar iconic monster roles to in-site White Box, Basic Fantasy RPG, and Basic Fantasy Field Guide replacements. It is intended as a quick conversion aid: linked names go to monster or subcategory pages, while italicized rows indicate substitutions that need heavier reskinning or added table rulings.

Iconic monster/familyClosest in-site equivalentsNotes
B. — laser-shooting eye tyrantOculus Rex; Great Orb of Eyes; Oculus Inferni / Oculus Abyssi / Oculus Somno / Oculus VigilansBest “floating eye tyrant / eye-ray boss” cluster.
G. — lesser eye monsterOculus Vigilans; Oculus Somno; Eye, Floating; Gaze GuardBest handled as lesser/specialized oculus-kin.
C.C. — tentacled corpse-eating crawlerTentacle Worm; Giant Centipede; MegalograptusTentacle Worm is the strongest swap: tentacles, paralysis, then feeding on helpless victims.
D.B. — false-position panther monsterDeceiver / Panther-Hydra; Blink Dog; AghlorvDeceiver is the strongest: black catlike monster whose mental power makes attackers misjudge its real location.
M.F. / I-d — brain-eating psychic octopusThulid; Thulid, Great Brain; Octopeople; Aboleth and SkumThulid is the direct replacement: squid-faced, brain-eating, telepathic, mind-blasting slaver.
U.H. — confusing-gaze tunnel bruteSyring / Syring Juggernaut; Aghar; Giant Boring Beetle; Purple WormNo perfect match. Use Syring for chitinous tunnel horror, Aghar for earth-moving brute, and add a confusion gaze if needed.
Y-t. — serpent cult infiltratorsSerpent Men / Greater Serpent Men; Ophidian; Spirit Naga / Guardian NagaSerpent Men are the strongest swap: ancient serpent rulers, infiltration, charm, priests, and sacrifices.
G-y. — astral sword raidersCelestial Egregor; Celestial Warden; Ogre Mage; Nazgorean Frogman/Nehnite faction reskinNo direct astral sword-raider culture. Use Celestials for extraplanar warrior-mystics or Ogre Mage for martial spellcaster raiders.
G-z. — planar ascetic mysticsMonk-ee; Celestial Watcher; Celestial OracleNo direct disciplined Limbo monastic analogue. Monk-ee covers the martial-arts side; Celestials cover the planar mystic side.
T-ri — chaos demon taxonomyInfernals; Balor; Glabrezu; Hezrou; Marilith; Nalfeshni; Vrock; Quasit; SuccubusTreat demons and infernals as the same lower-planar category; use the listed entries without preserving the tanar’ri taxonomy.
B-zu — lawful devil taxonomyInfernals; Barbed Devil; Bone Devil; Ice Devil; Imp; Lemure; Spined Devil; ErinyesTreat devil-like infernals and demons as the same lower-planar category; use the listed entries without preserving the baatezu taxonomy.
K-t. — fish-frog cultistsDeep Ones; Sahuagin; Locathah; Aboleth/SkumDeep Ones are the best fish-frog cultist swap; Sahuagin work better for militant aquatic raiders.
S-di — chaos-frog outsidersWug; Prince Frog; Giant Frog/Toad; HezrouNo direct chaos-frog outsider match. Use Wugs or Prince Frogs, then add planar chaos, mutation, or infection themes.
ModronsAdvi; Clockwork Skeleton; Clockwork Armature; Tin Golem / Household Golem / Purifier Golem / Rope Golem / Web GolemAdvi are the best “alien machine beings” swap; clockworks/golems work for construct hierarchy.
YugolothsInfernals broadly; Infernal Fiend; Eldri; Shadow Fiend; Night HagNo direct neutral-evil mercenary fiend taxonomy. File off the demon/devil flavor and run selected Infernals as contract fiends.
AarakocraAvir; Aeromi; Giant Eagle/Roc alliesAvir are the strongest swap: anthropomorphic bird-folk, mountain settlements, flight, aerial tactics, shamans, and magisters.
BullywugWug; Kappa/Suiko; Giant Frog/ToadWug is the clean frog-humanoid swap; Kappa/Suiko are better for drowning/grappling water-monster variants.
FlumphFlying Man-of-War; Giant Jellyfish; Gas Spore / Deep SporeGood visual/ecological swaps, but not a strong match for the benevolent/psychic flumph personality.
MyconidsFlorans; Ghost Shroom; Tentacled Shroom; Fungi, Violet (Medium)Use Florans for fungus/plant-folk society; use shrooms and fungi for the monster ecology.
FomorianCyclops; Hill Giant; Stone Giant; cursed giant reskinCyclops gives the one-eyed cursed giant angle; Hill/Stone Giant gives the brute chassis.
Galeb duhrAghar; Earth Elemental; Stone Golem; XornAghar or Earth Elemental is the best living-earth swap; Stone Golem works for guardian boulder-creatures.
GrellGiant Flying Brain; Flying Man-of-War; GrickNo one-piece match. Combine hovering brain horror with dangling paralyzing tentacles if you want the classic feel.
Hook HorrorSyring / Syring Juggernaut; Grawl; Giant Tuatara; Giant BeetleSyring gives subterranean chitinous brute energy, but the hook-handed cave predator needs reskinning.
QuaggothGrawl; Bugbear; Grimlock; SasquatchGrawl or Grimlock works for savage underground humanoids; Bugbear is the simplest chassis.
MorkothAboleth; Aquatic Naga; Kraken / Sea Monster; OctopeopleAboleth is best for aquatic lair-control and domination, but the morkoth’s hypnotic hoard-island niche needs added flavor.
DerroMoon Goblin; Grimlock; Grawl; Dwarf NPCs with madness/illusion magicNo direct mad subterranean dwarf. Start with dwarf/NPC or Moon Goblin stats and add paranoia, poison, or illusion magic.
