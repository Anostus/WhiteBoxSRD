Description

A Giant Seahorse is not closely related to a hippocampus, but is in fact an actual seahorse which grows almost to the size of a pony.

Though it is not very strong or graceful, it is sometimes used by undersea races to carry or pull things. It has infravision with a range of 120'. Some locathah train a small number of giant seahorses for messengers and scouts (these have a morale of 8).
