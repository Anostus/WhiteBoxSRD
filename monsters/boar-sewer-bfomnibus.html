Description

A Sewer Boar is a distant relative of the wild boar that lives underground in dungeons or sewers, but they can also be found in mud hollows, marshes, and swamps. Unlike regular boars, they grow up to 10 feet long and 5 feet tall at the shoulder. Sewer boars are covered in thick brown fur and have a layer of extraordinarily thick blubber.

Their blubber is so thick that any non-magical weapon will be stuck in the hide unless a save vs. Dragon Breath is made, with the character’s Strength modifier added. Sewer boars takes half-damage from all weapons, excepting spears and polearms, which can penetrate deeply enough to make it through the layer of blubber. A sewer boar can charge up to 90 feet, doing 1d12 points of damage to all creatures in the path of its charge, unless they succeed in a save vs. Death Ray to get out of the way.
