Stirge

Armor Class
5 [14]
Hit Dice
1-1
Attacks
Sting (1d6-1)
Special
Suck Blood
Move
15 while flying
HDE/XP
1/15

Description

These tiny birds have needle-like beaks which they use to impale their victims and then drain them of blood. Whenever a stirge makes a successful melee attack they remain attached to their target and automatically inflict 1d6-1 points of damage each round as they drink the victim’s blood.
