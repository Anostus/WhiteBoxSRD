Description

Medusae are horrid creatures from Greek mythology with a female face but hair of writhing snakes; they have no legs, but the body of a serpent.The gaze of a medusa turns anyone looking upon it into stone. In addition to the medusa’s relatively weak meleeweapon attack, the snake-hair makes one attack per round, causing no damage but lethally poisonous with a successful hit (saving throw applies).
