Race Index

White Box includes non-human character races at the Referee’s discretion. The Dwarf, Elf, and Halfling each have their own table-ready reference page.

The Dwarf

Underground folk with stonework sense, hardiness against magic, and skill against giants.

The Elf

Faerie or mythic folk who combine magic, swordplay, keen perception, and ancient enmities.

The Halfling

Small shire-folk with stealth, missile accuracy, and resilience against magic.

Race Tables

The Elf page includes the optional blended Fighter/Magic-user advancement table in context. The complete table collection is also available from Formatted Tables.
