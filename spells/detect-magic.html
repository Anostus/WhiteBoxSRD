Detect Magic

Cleric 1 Magic-User 1

Level
C1, M1
Range
60 ft.
Duration
20 min.

The caster can perceive in places, people, or things the presence of a magical spell or enchantment. Magical items or secretly placed charms may be discovered with this spell.
