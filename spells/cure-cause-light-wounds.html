Cure (Cause) Light Wounds

Cleric 1

Level
C1
Range
Touch
Duration
Instantaneous

As a Cure Light Wounds spell, the caster cures 1d6+1 HP.

As a Cause Light Wounds spell, the caster causes 1d6+1 damage.
