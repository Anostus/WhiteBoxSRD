Light (Dark), Continual

Cleric 3 Magic-User 2

Level
C3, M2
Range
120 ft.
Duration
Permanent until dispelled

As a Light, Continual spell, the caster targets a person or object which then produces a light about as bright as a torch with a radius of 20 feet.

As a Dark, Continual spell, the caster instead causes night-time darkness to fall upon the area with a radius of 20 feet.
