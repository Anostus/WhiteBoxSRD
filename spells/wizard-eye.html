Wizard Eye

Magic-User 4

Level
M4
Range
240 ft.
Duration
1 hour

The caster conjures up an invisible, magical “eye, ” that can move a maximum of 240 feet from its creator. It floats along as directed by the caster, at a rate of 120 feet per turn.
