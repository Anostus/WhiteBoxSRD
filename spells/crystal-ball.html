Crystal Ball

Magic-User 3

Level
M3
Range
60 ft.
Duration
2 hours

The caster must announce which of the two options is being cast. A two foot thickness of solid stone or a thin layer of lead blocks both options.

As a Clairaudience spell, the caster can hear through solid obstacles.

As a Clairvoyance spell, the caster can see through solid obstacles.
