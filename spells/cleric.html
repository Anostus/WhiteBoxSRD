Cleric Spell Index

Spells available to Clerics, grouped by spell level. Each name links to a dedicated spell page.

Level 1

  • Cure (Cause) Light Wounds Range Touch; Duration Instantaneous
  • Detect Chaos (Law) Range 120 ft. (C), 60 ft. (M); Duration 1 hour (C), 20 min. (M)
  • Detect Magic Range 60 ft.; Duration 20 min.
  • Light (Dark) Range 120 ft.; Duration 2 hours (C), 1 hour + 10 minutes per level (M)
  • Protection from Chaos (Law) Range Caster only; Duration 2 hours (C), 1 hour (M)
  • Purify (Putrefy) Food and Drink Range Close/Touch; Duration Instantaneous

Level 2

  • Bless (Curse) Range Target PC or NPC (out-ofcombat); Duration 1 hour
  • Find Traps Range 30 ft.; Duration 20 min.
  • Hold Person Range 180 ft. (C), 120 ft. (M); Duration 90 min. (C), 1 hour + 10 minutes per level (M)
  • Speak with Animals Range 30 ft.; Duration 1 hour

Level 3

  • Cure (Cause) Disease Range Touch; Duration Instantaneous
  • Light (Dark), Continual Range 120 ft.; Duration Permanent until dispelled
  • Locate Object Range 90 ft. (C), 60 ft. + 10 ft. per level (M); Duration 1 minutes per level
  • Remove Curse Range Close/Touch; Duration Instantaneous

Level 4

  • Cure (Cause) Serious Wounds Range Touch; Duration Instantaneous
  • Neutralize Poison Range Referee’s discretion; Duration 10 min.
  • Protection from Chaos (Law), 10 ft. radius Range 10 ft. radius around caster; Duration 2 hours
  • Speak with Plants Range 30 ft.; Duration 1 hour
  • Sticks to Snakes Range 120 ft.; Duration 1 hour

Level 5

  • Commune Range Caster; Duration 3 questions
  • Create Food and Drink Range Close; Duration Instantaneous
  • Dispel Chaos (Law) Range 30 ft.; Duration 10 minutes
  • Insect Plague Range 480 ft.; Duration 1 day
  • Quest Range 30 ft.; Duration Until completed
  • Raise Dead Range Line of sight; Duration See below
