Cure (Cause) Disease

Cleric 3

Level
C3
Range
Touch
Duration
Instantaneous

As a Cure Disease spell, the recipient is cured of all diseases—including those magically inflicted.

As a Cause Disease spell, the recipient is inflicted with a disease to be determined by the Referee.
