Charm Person

Magic-User 1

Level
M1
Range
120 ft.
Duration
Until dispelled

This spell affects living bipeds of human size or smaller, such as goblins or dryads. If the spell succeeds (saving throw allowed), the unfortunate creature falls under the caster’s influence.
