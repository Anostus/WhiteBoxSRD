Massmorph

Magic-User 4

Level
M4
Range
240 ft.
Duration
Until negated or dispelled

One hundred or fewer human-sized creatures are changed to appear like innocent trees. The illusion is so perfect that creatures moving through the “forest” will not detect the deception.
