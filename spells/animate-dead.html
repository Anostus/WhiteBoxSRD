Animate Dead

Magic-User 5

Level
M5
Range
Referee’s discretion
Duration
Permanent

This spell animates skeletons or zombies from dead bodies. 1d6 undead are animated (per level of the caster above 8th). The corpses remain animated until destroyed or dispelled.
