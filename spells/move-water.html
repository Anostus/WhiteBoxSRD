Move Water

Magic-User 6

Level
M6
Range
240 ft.
Duration
See below

The caster must announce which of the two options are being cast: The spell lowers the depth and water level of lakes, rivers, wells, and other bodies of water to 1/2 their normal levels. Used this way, the spell lasts for 10 turns. Alternately, this spell creates a gap through water, but only to a depth of 10 feet. Used this way, the spell lasts for 6 turns.
