Protection from Chaos (Law)

Cleric 1 Magic-User 1

Level
C1, M1
Range
Caster only
Duration
2 hours (C), 1 hour (M)

As a Protection from Chaos spell, the caster creates a magical field of protection around himself to block out all chaotic monsters, who suffer a -1 penalty “to-hit” against the caster, and the caster gains +1 on all saving throws against such attacks.

As a Protection from Law spell, it does the same thing except that lawful creatures suffer the -1 penalty.
